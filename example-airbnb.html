Welcome to Emberwood Social Club, your entertainment retreat in the Pocono Mountains designed for connection, fun, and unforgettable group getaways. Unwind in the private hot tub and sauna, challenge your friends in the arcade and game rooms, mix cocktails in the speakeasy lounge, or take the stage in your own karaoke room. Whether you're planning a ski trip, birthday celebration, friends’ getaway, or family vacation, Emberwood Social Club was designed to bring people together.

★★ About Towamensing Trails Community Amenities★★

Towamensing Trails is a private, wooded community in Albrightsville, PA, centered around a beautiful 190-acre lake with a sandy beach, a large outdoor swimming pool (open Memorial Day-Labor Day), tennis/pickleball/basketball/volleyball courts, a clubhouse with a restaurant/lounge, a playground, and a boat launch for electric motor or non-motorized boats. It offers a perfect blend of nature, recreation, and relaxation for your stay.
